How Beginners Are Launching Online Stores in 30 Minutes

Ecommerce is booming, and thousands of beginners are launching online stores every day without needing coding or technical experience.

One of the most popular platforms entrepreneurs use is Shopify, which allows anyone to create a professional online store in minutes.

Why Shopify?

  • No coding required
  • Hundreds of store templates
  • Secure payment processing
  • Easy product management
  • Works on desktop and mobile

How to Start

Starting an online store only takes a few simple steps:

  1. Create a Shopify account
  2. Choose your store theme
  3. Add your products
  4. Launch your store

Shopify currently offers a free trial so you can test the platform and build your store before paying anything.
